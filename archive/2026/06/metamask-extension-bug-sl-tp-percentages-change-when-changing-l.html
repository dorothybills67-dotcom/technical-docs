Metamask Extension Bug Sl Tp Percentages Change When Changing L

Symptom

  • Users of the Metamask extension on the Ethereum (ETH) ecosystem may observe that their Stop-Loss (SL) and Take-Profit (TP) percentages unexpectedly change when they adjust the leverage (L) in their trading settings.

Technical Root Cause

  • The issue arises due to a bug within the Metamask extension that incorrectly recalculates SL and TP percentages based on the changed leverage, rather than maintaining the originally set values.

Manual Overrides

  • To temporarily resolve this issue, users can attempt the following manual overrides:
  • Manually re-enter the desired SL and TP percentages after adjusting the leverage.
  • Utilize a third-party trading interface that does not rely on the Metamask extension for SL and TP management.
  • Execute a script to automatically adjust the SL and TP percentages back to their original values after leverage changes:
#!/bin/bash
# Define original SL and TP percentages
ORIGINAL_SL=5
ORIGINAL_TP=10

# Adjust SL and TP after leverage change
LEV_CHANGE_EVENT=$(lev_change_detected)
if [ $LEV_CHANGE_EVENT == "true" ]; then
  # Reset SL and TP to original percentages
  set_sl_tp $ORIGINAL_SL $ORIGINAL_TP
fi
