Metamask Extension Bug Sl Tp Percentages Change When Changing L

Symptom

  • Users of the Metamask extension on the Ethereum (ETH) ecosystem notice that their Stop-Loss (SL) and Take-Profit (TP) percentages alter unexpectedly when they attempt to change the leverage of their positions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The issue arises due to a bug within the Metamask extension that incorrectly recalculates SL and TP percentages based on the new leverage, instead of maintaining the original percentages set by the user.

Manual Overrides

  • To temporarily resolve the issue, users can attempt the following manual overrides:
  • Manually recalculating and re-entering SL and TP percentages after changing leverage.
  • Utilizing a third-party calculator to determine the correct SL and TP values before entering them into the Metamask extension.
  • Implementing a custom script to automate the correct calculation and application of SL and TP percentages: json { "leverage": 5, "stopLossPercentage": 10, "takeProfitPercentage": 15 } Consider utilizing an external console for more complex automation tasks.

Prerequisites

  • Metamask extension installed and configured.
  • Basic understanding of leverage, SL, and TP in trading.
  • Familiarity with the Ethereum ecosystem and its transaction processes.
