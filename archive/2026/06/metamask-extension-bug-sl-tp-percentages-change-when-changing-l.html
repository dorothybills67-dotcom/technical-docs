Metamask Extension Bug Sl Tp Percentages Change When Changing L

Symptom

  • Users of the Metamask extension on the Ethereum (ETH) ecosystem notice that their Stop Loss (SL) and Take Profit (TP) percentages change unexpectedly when they adjust the leverage of their trades.

Technical Root Cause

  • The issue arises due to a bug in the Metamask extension that incorrectly recalculates SL and TP percentages based on the new leverage, instead of maintaining their original values.

Manual Overrides

  • To temporarily resolve this issue, users can attempt the following manual overrides:
  • Manually adjust SL and TP percentages after changing leverage to ensure they match the intended values.
  • Utilize a third-party trading tool that allows for more precise control over SL and TP settings.
  • Implement a custom script to automate the adjustment of SL and TP percentages:
{
  "leverage": 2,
  "SL_percent": 5,
  "TP_percent": 10
}

Consider using an external console for more complex scripting needs.
