Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a backlog in blob space allocation.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, specifically when the demand for blob space exceeds the available capacity, causing a delay in processing transactions.

    "gasPrice": "20 Gwei"

}
