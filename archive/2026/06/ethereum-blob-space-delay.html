Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues related to excess data storage.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the Ethereum network's limited capacity for storing and processing large amounts of data, particularly in the form of blobs. When the network is congested, or if there's an excessive demand for data storage, it can lead to delays in processing transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transaction processing: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '0x...', gas: '0x...'})
  • Optimize data storage by compressing or splitting large files:
curl -X POST \
  http://localhost:8545 \

  • Utilize layer 2 scaling solutions or off-chain storage to reduce the burden on the Ethereum network
