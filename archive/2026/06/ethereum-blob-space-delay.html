Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or pending transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a blob space delay, resulting in slower-than-expected processing times for their transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, where the available blob space is insufficient to handle the high volume of transactions, leading to a delay in processing.
