Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a backlog in blob space processing.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, where the demand for blob space exceeds the available capacity, causing a delay in processing transactions.

Manual Overrides
