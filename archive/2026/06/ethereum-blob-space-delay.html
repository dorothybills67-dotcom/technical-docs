Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar wording, which suggests that the network is currently overwhelmed and cannot process transactions at the expected speed.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the Ethereum network's current limitations in handling a high volume of transactions, particularly those involving large amounts of data such as NFTs or complex smart contracts. When the network is congested, transactions may be delayed or fail as the network struggles to keep up with demand.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas price to prioritize the transaction: this can help the transaction be processed faster, but at a higher cost.
  • Use a transaction accelerator service: some services offer the ability to accelerate transactions for a fee.
  • Edit the transaction manually using a command-line tool:
eth_sendTransaction '{ 
  "from": "0x...your_address...", 
  "to": "0x...recipient_address...", 
  "value": "0x...amount...", 
  "gas": "0x...gas...", 
  "gasPrice": "0x...gas_price..." 
}'

You may want to consider using an external console with more advanced features to diagnose and resolve the issue.

Prerequisites

  • Basic understanding of Ethereum transactions and gas mechanics
  • Access to a command-line tool or Ethereum wallet software
  • Knowledge of the Ethereum network's current congestion and gas price trends
