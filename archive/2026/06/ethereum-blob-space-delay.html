Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues related to data storage and retrieval.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network's blob space, which is used for storing and managing large data blobs. This congestion can be caused by a high volume of transactions, inadequate gas prices, or inefficient data storage practices.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas price to prioritize transactions and alleviate network congestion.
  • Optimize data storage by using efficient encoding and compression methods.
  • Utilize layer 2 scaling solutions to offload data storage and processing from the main Ethereum network.
