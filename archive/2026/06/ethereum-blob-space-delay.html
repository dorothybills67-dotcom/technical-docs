Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a backlog in blob space processing, resulting in slow or stalled transaction confirmations.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network's blob space, which is a storage mechanism for large data blobs. This congestion can be caused by a high volume of transactions, particularly those involving large amounts of data, overwhelming the network's capacity to process and store these blobs in a timely manner.

Manual Overrides
