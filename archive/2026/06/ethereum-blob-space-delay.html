Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues, resulting in frustrated users and potential financial losses.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the Ethereum network's limited capacity to process large amounts of data, specifically with regards to blob spaces, which are used to store and manage data on the blockchain, leading to congestion and delays.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions: eth_sendTransaction {from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '0x...', gas: '0x...' }
  • Use a different blob space provider to distribute the load
  • Optimize data storage to reduce the size of blob spaces
