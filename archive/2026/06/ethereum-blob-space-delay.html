Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or pending transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a blob space delay. The delay affects the overall performance and efficiency of the network, causing frustration among users.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an overwhelming number of unprocessed transactions, causing a backlog in the Ethereum network's blob space. This backlog leads to delayed transaction processing times, resulting in a poor user experience.
