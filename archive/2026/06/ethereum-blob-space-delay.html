Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a delay in the Ethereum blob space. The delay can cause transactions to remain in a pending state for an extended period.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs when the Ethereum network is congested, causing a backlog of transactions waiting to be processed. This congestion can be due to a high volume of transactions, network issues, or limitations in the Ethereum protocol.

Manual Overrides

  • To resolve the issue, try the following manual overrides:
  • Increase the gas price to prioritize the transaction
  • Use a different Ethereum node or provider to reduce congestion
