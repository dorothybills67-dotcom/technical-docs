Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a backlog in the blob space, resulting in increased transaction processing times.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network's blob space, which is responsible for storing and managing large data blobs associated with transactions, leading to slower transaction processing and confirmations.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions and reduce delay: eth_estimateGas
  • Utilize layer 2 scaling solutions to offload transactions and reduce network congestion
  • Optimize transaction batching to minimize the number of transactions competing for space in the blob
