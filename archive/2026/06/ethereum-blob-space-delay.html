Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, often accompanied by an error message indicating a blob space delay. The delay manifests as a prolonged processing time for transactions, resulting in slower than expected confirmation times.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to the network's limited capacity to process and store large amounts of data, particularly with the introduction of blob-related transactions. When the network is congested, transactions that involve large data blobs are prioritized lower, leading to delays.

Manual Overrides

  • To mitigate the delay, users can attempt the following:
  • Increase the gas price to prioritize the transaction
  • Split large transactions into smaller ones to reduce network load
  • Utilize a custom transaction batching script, such as:

  • Access to a compatible Ethereum wallet or transaction tool
