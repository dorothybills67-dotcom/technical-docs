Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a backlog in the blob space, resulting in slower than expected transaction processing times.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an overload of the Ethereum network, causing a delay in the availability of blob space, which is necessary for storing and retrieving data associated with transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Adjust gas prices to prioritize transactions: Increase the gas price to incentivize miners to process transactions more quickly.
  • Utilize layer 2 scaling solutions: Implement solutions like Optimism or Polygon to reduce the load on the main Ethereum network.
  • Modify transaction batching:
