Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues related to data storage and retrieval.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the Ethereum network's limitations in handling large amounts of data, specifically with the storage and retrieval of blobs (binary large objects). When the network is congested or experiencing high demand, it can lead to delays in processing transactions that involve large data payloads.

Manual Overrides

  • Check the current network congestion and gas prices to determine the best time for transaction submission.
  • Optimize data storage by using compression or splitting large files into smaller, more manageable chunks.
  • Utilize layer 2 scaling solutions or off-chain storage options to reduce the burden on the Ethereum network.
