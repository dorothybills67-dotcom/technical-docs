Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues, resulting in slower than expected processing times for their transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the Ethereum network's current limitation in handling large amounts of data, specifically with the blob space, which is used for storing and managing data associated with transactions. When the network is congested, this can lead to delays.
