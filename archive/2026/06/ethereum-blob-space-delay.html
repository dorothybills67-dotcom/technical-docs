Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a blob space delay. The delay affects the overall performance and efficiency of the network, causing frustration among users.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to excessive blob data being stored on the Ethereum network, leading to slow processing times and delays. This is often caused by an increase in network activity, large file transfers, or inefficient data storage practices.

Manual Overrides
