Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or pending transactions on the Ethereum network, resulting in failed blob space allocations, with error messages indicating a delay in blob space processing.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to network congestion, high gas prices, or insufficient blob space allocation, causing a backlog in transaction processing and delaying the allocation of blob space.

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transaction processing: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '0x...', gas: '0x...'})
