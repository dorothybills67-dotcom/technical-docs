Symptom

  • Users experience delays in Ethereum transactions, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" on the Ethereum network.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a temporary backlog in the Ethereum blob storage system, which is used for storing and retrieving data associated with transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize the transaction: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '1 ether', gas: '2000000'})
  • Adjust the transaction nonce to bypass stuck transactions:
geth console --exec "eth.sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '1 ether', nonce: '10'})"
  • Use a third-party service to monitor and manage transaction queues for optimal performance

For optimal results, consider using an external console for advanced troubleshooting.

Prerequisites

  • Ethereum node (geth or parity) installed and running
