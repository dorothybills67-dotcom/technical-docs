Symptom

  • Users experience delayed blob space allocation on the Ethereum network, resulting in slower than expected transaction processing times and potential timeouts.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to high network congestion and insufficient gas prices, leading to a backlog of pending transactions and slow allocation of blob space.
