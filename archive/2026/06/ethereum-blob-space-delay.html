Symptom

  • Users experience delayed transactions and high gas fees on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues, resulting in slow processing times for their transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the Ethereum network's limitations in handling large amounts of data, particularly with the introduction of blob-related transactions, which consume more space on the blockchain and slow down the verification process.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions: eth_estimateGas
  • Use a third-party service to batch transactions and reduce network load: 
curl -X POST \
  https://mainnet.infura.io/v3/YOUR_PROJECT_ID \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  -d '{"jsonrpc":"2.0","method":"eth_sendTransaction","params":[{"from":"0x...","to":"0x...","value":"0x..."}],"id":1}'
  • Configure a local Ethereum node to manually manage transaction verification and gas pricing

Prerequisites: * An Ethereum wallet with a sufficient balance * A basic understanding of Ethereum transaction mechanics * Access to a reliable Ethereum node or third-party service

Consider utilizing an external console to streamline the troubleshooting process and execute the above commands efficiently.
