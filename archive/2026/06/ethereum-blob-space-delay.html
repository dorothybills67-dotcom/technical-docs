Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed blob space allocation on the Ethereum network, resulting in slower transaction processing times and potential timeouts.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to high network congestion, causing a backlog in blob space allocation, which is a critical component for efficient data storage and retrieval on the Ethereum blockchain.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit for transactions to prioritize blob space allocation:
  • eth_estimateGas to estimate the required gas
  • eth_sendTransaction with increased gas limit
  • eth_getTransactionReceipt to verify transaction status

  • Familiarity with Ethereum JSON-RPC API
