Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating an Ethereum blob space delay. This delay can cause frustration and impact the overall usability of the network.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the limited blob space available on the Ethereum network, which can lead to congestion and delays in transaction processing. This limitation is a result of the network's design and the increasing demand for blob space.
