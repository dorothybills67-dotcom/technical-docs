Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating an Ethereum blob space delay. This issue manifests as prolonged processing times for transactions, causing inconvenience and potential financial losses.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs when the network is congested, and the available blob space is insufficient to process transactions efficiently. This congestion can be caused by a high volume of transactions, large transaction sizes, or network configuration issues.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '0x...', gas: '0x...'})
  • Optimize transaction batching to reduce network load
  • Implement a queue system to manage transactions and alleviate congestion
