Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating an "Ethereum blob space delay". This issue is characterized by slow processing times and potential timeouts for transactions that rely on blob space.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network's blob space, which is used for storing and retrieving large data blobs associated with transactions. This congestion can be caused by a high volume of transactions competing for limited space, leading to delays and failures.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit for transactions to prioritize them over others and potentially bypass the congestion.
  • Implement a queueing system to manage and prioritize transactions, reducing the load on the blob space.

{
  "gas": "2000000",
  "gasPrice": "20.0",
  "blobSpacePriority": true
}

Prerequisites
