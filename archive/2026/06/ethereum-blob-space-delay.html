Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar phrases, which may be accompanied by increased transaction fees and slower block confirmation times.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to the network's limited capacity to process and store large amounts of data, particularly when there is a high volume of transactions competing for space in each block, thereby causing congestion and delays.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '1 ether', gas: '2000000'})
  • Utilize layer 2 scaling solutions to offload transactions from the mainnet
  • Implement a queue system to manage and prioritize transactions, example:

Prerequisites
