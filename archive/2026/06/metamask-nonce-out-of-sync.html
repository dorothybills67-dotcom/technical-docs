Metamask Nonce Out Of Sync

Symptom

  • Users encounter an error when attempting to send a transaction, with MetaMask displaying a "Nonce out of sync" error message, preventing the transaction from being processed.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a discrepancy between the expected nonce value stored in MetaMask and the actual nonce value on the Ethereum blockchain, often caused by multiple pending transactions or a mismatch in the transaction count.
