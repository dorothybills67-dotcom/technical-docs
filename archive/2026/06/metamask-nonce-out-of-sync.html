Symptom

  • The user encounters a "Nonce out of sync" error when attempting to send a transaction using MetaMask on the Ethereum (ETH) network, indicating a discrepancy between the expected and actual transaction count.

Technical Root Cause

  • This error occurs when MetaMask's understanding of the user's transaction count (nonce) does not match the actual nonce value on the Ethereum blockchain, often due to pending or failed transactions that have not been properly accounted for.

Manual Overrides

  • Reset MetaMask account: This involves removing and re-adding the account to MetaMask to resynchronize the nonce.
  • Clear pending transactions: Users can attempt to clear any pending transactions that may be causing the nonce discrepancy.
  • Update nonce manually: In some cases, manually updating the nonce to match the current blockchain state may resolve the issue.
