Symptom

  • Users encounter a "TronLink bandwidth error" when attempting to perform transactions or interact with the TRON network using TronLink, a popular wallet and browser extension.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to insufficient bandwidth or energy on the TRON network, which is required to process transactions. This can be caused by a high volume of transactions, network congestion, or insufficient TRX (TRON's native cryptocurrency) holdings to cover the energy costs.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase TRX holdings to cover energy costs: Ensure you have sufficient TRX in your wallet to cover the energy costs of transactions.
  • Adjust transaction settings: Modify transaction settings to optimize energy consumption.
