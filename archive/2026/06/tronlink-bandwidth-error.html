Tronlink Bandwidth Error

Symptom

  • Users encounter a "Tronlink Bandwidth Error" message when attempting to perform transactions or interact with smart contracts on the TRON network, indicating insufficient bandwidth for the operation.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the TRON network's bandwidth mechanism, which requires users to have a certain amount of bandwidth (measured in bandwidth points) to perform transactions. If a user's available bandwidth is depleted, they must wait for it to recharge or acquire more through freezing TRX.

Manual Overrides

  • Freeze additional TRX to increase bandwidth points: This can be done through the TRON wallet or exchange.
  • Wait for bandwidth to recharge: Bandwidth recharges over time, allowing for a limited number of free transactions per day.
  • Use a bandwidth delegation service: Some services allow users to delegate their unused bandwidth to others in exchange for a fee.
