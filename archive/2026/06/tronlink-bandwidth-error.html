Symptom

  • Users encounter a "TronLink bandwidth error" when attempting to perform transactions or interact with the TRON network through TronLink, a popular wallet and browser extension.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to insufficient bandwidth or energy on the TRON network, which is required to process transactions and execute smart contracts. This can be caused by a variety of factors, including high network congestion, insufficient TRX holdings, or incorrect configuration of bandwidth and energy settings.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the bandwidth and energy limits in the TronLink settings to allocate more resources for transaction processing.
  • Use the TRON command-line interface to manually override bandwidth settings with the following command:
tronweb.transactionBuilder.freezeBalance('TRX', 10000)
  • Verify that the TRX balance is sufficient to cover the required bandwidth and energy costs for the desired transactions.

Prerequisites: * TRX balance greater than 0 * TronLink wallet installed and configured * TRON network connection established

For advanced troubleshooting, consider using an external console to execute complex commands and diagnose network issues.
