Symptom

  • Users of the Metamask extension on the Avalanche network may notice that their balance for the Aave v3 WAVAX token is incorrectly displayed, potentially leading to confusion and incorrect transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a miscommunication between the Metamask extension and the Aave v3 protocol on the Avalanche network, resulting in an incorrect balance being fetched and displayed to the user.

Manual Overrides
