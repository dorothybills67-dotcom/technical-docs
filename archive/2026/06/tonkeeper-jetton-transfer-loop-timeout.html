Symptom

  • Users experience a "Tonkeeper Jetton transfer loop timeout" error when attempting to transfer Jettons, resulting in failed transactions and potential losses.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a misconfiguration in the Tonkeeper wallet settings or a temporary network congestion issue, causing the transfer loop to timeout.

Manual Overrides

  • Check the Tonkeeper wallet settings to ensure that the gas price and gas limit are set correctly:
  • Verify the Ton blockchain network connection stability
  • Check for any pending transactions that may be causing the timeout
  • Adjust the transfer loop timeout settings using the following command:
tonos-cli call -s <wallet_address> --abi TonkeeperWallet.abi set_timeout <new_timeout_value>

Consider utilizing an external console for advanced debugging.

Prerequisites

  • Tonos-cli installed and configured on the system
  • Tonkeeper wallet and Jetton contract ABIs available
  • Basic understanding of the TON blockchain and Tonkeeper wallet functionality
