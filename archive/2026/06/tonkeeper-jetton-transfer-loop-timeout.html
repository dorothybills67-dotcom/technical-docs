Symptom

  • The user encounters a "Tonkeeper Jetton transfer loop timeout" error when attempting to transfer jettons, indicating a failure in the transfer process due to a timeout.

Technical Root Cause

  • This error occurs due to the Tonkeeper wallet's inability to complete the jetton transfer within the specified time frame, often resulting from network congestion, high gas fees, or issues with the smart contract.

Manual Overrides

  • To resolve the issue, try the following manual overrides:
  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize the transaction
  • Reduce the transfer amount to minimize network load
  • Utilize an alternative transfer method, such as a different wallet or exchange
