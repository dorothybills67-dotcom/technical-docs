Symptom

  • Users of the TON ecosystem may encounter an error message indicating a "Tonkeeper Jetton transfer loop timeout" when attempting to transfer jettons, resulting in failed transactions and potential losses.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a timeout in the Tonkeeper Jetton transfer loop, which can be caused by network congestion, high gas fees, or issues with the smart contract responsible for facilitating the transfer.

Manual Overrides

  • To troubleshoot the issue, users can attempt the following:
  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize the transaction
  • Reduce the transaction amount to minimize network fees
  • Utilize an alternative transfer method, such as a different wallet or exchange
