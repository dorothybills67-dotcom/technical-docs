Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • Users experience a sudden and significant increase in energy consumption when attempting to transfer TRC-20 tokens on the TRON network, resulting in a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" message.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in network activity, causing a sharp increase in energy consumption, which exceeds the allocated energy limits for the user's account.

Manual Overrides

  • To resolve the issue, try the following manual overrides:
  • Increase the account's energy limit by submitting a request to the network administrators.
  • Use a different wallet or account with a higher energy allocation.
  • Implement a custom energy management script, such as:
{
  "account": "user_account",
  "energy_limit": 1000,
  "script": "energy_manager"
}

Consider utilizing an external console for more advanced troubleshooting and configuration options.

Prerequisites

  • TRON network account with sufficient energy allocation
  • TRC-20 token wallet with transfer functionality
  • Basic understanding of TRON network architecture and energy management principles
