Technical Root Cause

  • This error occurs when a TRC-20 smart contract exceeds its allocated energy limit, causing the contract execution to fail and resulting in the error message.

  • Adjust the contract's energy limit by modifying the energyLimit parameter in the contract's configuration.

  "energyFee": 1
}
