Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • Users encounter a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" message when attempting to initiate transactions on the TRON network, resulting in failed transfers and smart contract executions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy consumption by TRC-20 tokens, exceeding the allocated energy limits for user accounts, causing the network to reject transactions and prevent further energy depletion.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the energy limit for the user account using the tronweb interface:
  • Update account permissions to include higher energy allowances
  • Utilize the tronweb.trx.freeze function to temporarily increase energy capacity
  • Execute the following command to adjust energy settings:
tronweb.trx.updateAccount(
  'account_address',
  {
    'account_name': 'new_account_name',
    'energy_limit': 10000
  }
)

Consider utilizing a separate console for advanced debugging.

Prerequisites

  • TRON network account with sufficient TRX balance
  • tronweb library installed and configured
  • Basic understanding of TRON network and TRC-20 token functionality
