Symptom

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy consumption by a smart contract, exceeding the maximum allowed energy limit for a single transaction, causing the TRON Virtual Machine (TVM) to terminate the transaction and return the error.

Manual Overrides
