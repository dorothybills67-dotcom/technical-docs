Symptom

  • Users encounter a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" when attempting to initiate transactions or interact with smart contracts on the TRON network, resulting in failed operations and potential loss of assets.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a sudden and significant increase in energy consumption by a TRC-20 token contract, exceeding the allocated energy limits and causing the contract to fail.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the energy limit for the specific TRC-20 token contract using the TRON Web Wallet or TronLink.
  • Optimize contract code to reduce energy consumption and prevent future spikes.
  • Manually adjust the contract's energy parameters using the following command:
tronweb.transactionBuilder.triggerSmartContract(
  "contract_address", 
  "function_name", 
  {"parameter": "value"}, 
  1000000
)

Consider utilizing an external console for more complex contract adjustments.

Prerequisites

  • TRON Web Wallet or TronLink installed and configured.
