Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • Users encounter a sudden and significant increase in energy consumption when attempting to transfer TRC-20 tokens on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and error messages indicating a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error".

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy demand, often caused by a large number of concurrent transactions or malicious activities on the network, overwhelming the available energy resources.

Manual Overrides

  • To mitigate the issue, try the following:
  • Increase the transaction energy limit using the --energy flag.
  • Adjust the node configuration to prioritize energy allocation for critical transactions.
  • Implement a custom energy management script, such as:
{
  "energy": {
    "limit": 1000,
    "priority": "high"
  }
}
