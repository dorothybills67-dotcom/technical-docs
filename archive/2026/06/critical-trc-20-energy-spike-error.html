Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • Users experience a sudden and drastic increase in energy consumption when interacting with TRC-20 tokens on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and error messages indicating a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error".

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy demand, often caused by inefficient smart contract code, network congestion, or excessive transaction volume.
