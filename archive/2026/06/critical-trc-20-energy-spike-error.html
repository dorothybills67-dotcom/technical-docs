Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • The user encounters a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" message when attempting to perform a transaction on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and potential losses.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy consumption by a smart contract, exceeding the allowed limits and causing the transaction to fail.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the energy limit for the transaction: --energy 1000
  • Optimize the smart contract code to reduce energy consumption: 
{
  "contract": "example",
  "functions": [
    {
      "name": "energyIntensiveFunction",
      "energy": 500
    }
  ]
}
  • Utilize a different smart contract with more efficient energy usage: tronweb.contract.at('newContractAddress')

Prerequisites: * TRONWeb installed and configured * Familiarity with TRON's smart contract ecosystem * Access to the TRON network

Consider utilizing an external console, such as TronBox, for more advanced debugging and troubleshooting capabilities.
