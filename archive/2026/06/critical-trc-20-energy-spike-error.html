Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • Users experience a sudden and significant increase in energy consumption when interacting with TRC-20 tokens on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and error messages indicating a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error".

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an imbalance in the network's energy allocation, often caused by a surge in demand for TRC-20 transactions, exceeding the available energy capacity of the network.

Manual Overrides

  • Adjust the transaction energy limit using the --energy flag when sending transactions.
  • Implement a queue system to stagger transactions and prevent energy spikes.
  • Utilize the following JSON configuration to override default energy settings:
{
  "energy": {
    "limit": 1000,
    "ratio": 0.5
  }
}
