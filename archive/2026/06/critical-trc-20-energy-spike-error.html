Symptom

  • The user encounters a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" message, indicating an abnormal increase in energy consumption by a TRC-20 smart contract on the TRON network.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy usage, often caused by inefficient contract code, excessive loop iterations, or an unusually high volume of transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Adjust the contract's energy limit to prevent excessive consumption:
  • Increase the energyLimit parameter in the contract deployment script
  • Optimize contract code to reduce computational complexity
  • Implement rate limiting to throttle transaction volumes
tronweb.txBuilder.createSmartContract(
  "contract_address",
  "contract_abi",
  {
    "feeLimit": 100000000,
    "energyLimit": 100000000
  }
)

Prerequisites

  • Familiarity with TRON's smart contract development framework
  • Understanding of energy consumption mechanics on the TRON network
  • Access to the TRONWeb library for contract deployment and management
